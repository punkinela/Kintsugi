## ‚úÖ **Implemented: AI-Powered Accomplishment Analysis**

### What Was Added
Your journal now includes intelligent analysis that:
- **Auto-categorizes** accomplishments (Leadership, Technical, Communication, etc.)
- **Extracts skills** mentioned in your text (Python, Project Management, etc.)
- **Assesses impact level** (Low, Medium, High, Exceptional)
- **Identifies strength areas** (Problem Solving, Teamwork, etc.)
- **Provides suggestions** to make accomplishments more impactful
- **Generates insights** about your overall accomplishment profile
- **Exports to Markdown** for easy sharing and portfolio building

### How It Works
1. **Write your accomplishment** in the journal
2. **Click "Analyze with AI"** to get instant feedback
3. **See categories, skills, and impact** automatically detected
4. **Get suggestions** to strengthen your description
5. **View insights** about patterns across all accomplishments
6. **Export everything** as a professional document
